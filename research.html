Interests

I like to study non-perturbative aspects of supersymmetric field theories using geometry and representation theory. This typically involves thinking about the mathematical structures of BPS states and local / extended operators.
I am currently interested in the relation between categories of line operators in the holomorphic-topological twist of 4d N=2 theories and algebras of BPS states.
